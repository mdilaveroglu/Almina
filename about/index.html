PASSION. QUALITY. FLAVOR

Almina Trading brings the finest spreads to your table, blending quality ingredients with a passion for exceptional taste.

We as Almina Trading are manufacturers of hazelnut chocolate spread. Our factory is based in Durban, South Africa. Originally a Turkish company, operating out of Istanbul, we moved our factory to South Africa and have been operating here since 2016.

 

Almina Trading has the capacity to produce a hundred tons of chocolate spread and Baking chocolate per month. We are currently producing under our own brand Suflenut. We are also the suppliers to multiple bakeries and retailers in South Africa.

 

Our range of chocolate spreads is milk chocolate, white chocolate, dark chocolate, chocolate spread with hazelnut, and caramel spread. The chocolate can be used for either baking with, in the cake batter, in the frosting or as a ganache. It has been used to glaze and stuff doughnuts and croissants, as well as on and in ice-cream. It is also enjoyed as a chocolate spread on bread at home. Our products are of excellent quality with reasonable pricing.

We can and have customize the product as per a customers demand, i.e. percentage of cocoa, hazelnut and or chosen aroma. As well as have your own branding.
